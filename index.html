How

How It Works

Enecuum is the first blockchain in the world to aggregate all connected devices into a blockchain network that utilizes this untapped data processing capacity. The more devices are connected to the network, the higher the speed. There is no cap to scalability. Millions of devices scattered around the world create a truly decentralized, safe, and stable system.

Learn More

Phone

Phone Mining

People only use less than 15% of data processing capacity of their smartphone. Enecuum leases this unused capacity and in return rewards the device owner. The smartphone becomes a part of a huge network, with a special Trinity algorithm using the capacity of each device in the most effective way.

Every smartphone on the network will participate in the publication of microblocks on a daily basis and receive a reward for this. All you need to do is  download the Enecuum App.

Mission

Our Mission

Enecuum allows each smartphone owner to participate in a global network and get rewarded for it. Crypto technologies should not belong to a small community of technological elite. On the contrary, everyone should have the opportunity to become a part of a beautiful future. We make access to this advanced technology simple and easy. Open Tomorrow now.

World

Changing The World

Enecuum is an ideal platform for companies that needs a fast secure, and decentralized blockchain. We are pleasedto have created atechnology that can radically ease various areas of life and business: from providing free mobile communication for subscribers to puting scientific breakthroughs within reach with unlimited computing capacity
Education

Fake diplomas and certificates, fake academic degrees – these problems are still faced by the scientific community. Enecuum will provide transparency and authenticity of the documents. They cannot be rewritten or partially modified. Records will be stored in a decentralized network and will be accessible to everyone from anywhere in the world.

Telecom

Enecuum can create opportunity for free mobile communication for subscribers and thousands of new customers for mobile operators. Enecuum opens new horizons for the Telecom sector. For the first time in history, the smartphone will actually generate passive income for its owner.

Science

The computing power of Enecuum can be used to solve complex scientific problems, with the results available to any international scientific community.

Medicine

You will now be able to get a prescription for a medicine in Argentina and purchase this medicine in Italy. Enecuum has made this possible thanks to our single decentralized storage network for your health records. This kind of global access never existed before, due to technical limitations. We at Enecuum have removed those limitations forever.

Government

Enecuum facilitates voting of any degree of complexity, and protects the voting results from falsification. The world has already seen elections take place using blockchain technology on an experimental basis. The outcomes of these experiments were found to be successful.

Software

Thanks to Enecuum, developers gain the opportunity to implement distributed applications of any complexity, without having to worry about the power of their physical servers.

Logistic

Enecuum technology allows you to easily and safely manage the delivery of goods around the world. Logistics has never been so easy and convenient.

Insurance

Enecuum can provide the facility of individual calculation of insurance policies for each client, o ering both full transparency and the inability to "cheat" the system on the part of either the policy provider or the client. Our technology will reduce the cost of insurance for the client while protecting insurance companies from fraudsters.
